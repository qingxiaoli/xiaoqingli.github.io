李晓晴 / Xiaoqing Li

自2021年起，我任职于首都经济贸易大学数据科学系，担任讲师。此前，我于2021年获得北京大学数学学院理学博士学位，师从杨建省教授与马尽问教授。我的本科教育则就读于中国海洋大学数学科学学院，并于2016年取得理学学士学位。目前，我的研究兴趣主要集中在人工智能、计算机视觉和机器学习等领域。

Since 2021, I have been a Lecturer in the Department of Data Science at Capital University of Economics and Business. Prior to this, I received my Ph.D. in Science from the School of Mathematical Sciences, Peking University in 2021, under the supervision of Professor Jiansheng Yang and Professor Jinwen Ma. I obtained my Bachelor of Science degree from the School of Mathematical Sciences, Ocean University of China in 2016. Currently, my research interests primarily lie in the areas of Artificial Intelligence, Computer Vision, and Machine Learning.
